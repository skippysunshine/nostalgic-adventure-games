I can't say it enough, people! If you haven't installed the V1.03 patch from the Ubisoft website, none of the following will help you.

Here are the 'calls' and 'names' for each of the mangrees. A long rectangle respresents one full turn of the wheel (maybe even a little over), a square represents a 1/3 turn of the relevant wheel. The calls are aligned to either the left, middle or right wheel. Just turn the wheels in a relaxed manner - you don't need to turn them quickly.


         

Floppy Ears

Grandpa

Long Ears

One Ear

Grandpa Throw

         

The following instructions are based on the numberings in the picture above. Don't forget that a long call is one full turn of a wheel and a short call is a 1/3 turn.

Don't worry if you dont always hear the sound turning the wheel makes - this seems to be an audio glitch as the mangrees still react if you have turned the wheels correctly.

You can reset the puzzle by walking back and going inside Achenar's hut on the lagoon. As you complete a step correctly, you should save your game. There is no time limit for the first 8 steps below.
