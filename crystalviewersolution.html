The slider has nine positions (first position is 1).

There are 5 compartments with a button each and a larger orange button in front.

A lever can be rotated to select from 6 colours.



Move the slider to Position 7 and leave the lever below over the red dot
Press the button in front of compartment 1.

Move the slider to Position 4 and move the lever below to the cyan dot.
Press the button in front of compartment 2

Move the slider to Position 9 and move the lever below to the yellow dot.
Press the button in front of compartment 3

Leave the slider in Position 9 and move the lever below over the green dot
Press the button in front of compartment 4.

Move the slider to Position 3 and move the lever below to the blue dot.
Press the button in front of compartment 5
