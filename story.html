The Characters
     
     
 
 
Atrus

Patriach of the family - a man of great vision and creativity. Like many genius', he missed out on the common sense gene, and is incapable of correcting any of his mistakes without your help.
Yeesha

Youngest child of Atrus and Catherine. Pretentious, righteous and aside from the whole homicidal siblings thing, is living one of the best childhoods I could imagine!
Catherine

Matriarch of the family and apparently still alive - although you wouldn't know it by playing Revelation. I still find her complete absence suspicious.
 
 
Sirrus

Talk about the most severe case of 'middle child syndrome' ever! He's evil, he's evil. Why would you ever think he is not? His slogan should be..... "If it wasn't for you pesky kids...."
Alice

I was always surprised that for a show made in the very early 70's, nobody minded that Alice was having casual sex in her employer's home with the local butcher.
Achenar

Okay, so he's probably not very popular amongst vegetarians but he turns out to be a good guy (albeit a good, dead guy).

Memories on Yeesha's Necklace
 
I couldn't find a complete list anywhere of all the memories available on Yeesha's necklace so I've had a go at doing one here. Please let me know if you can see that any are missing or incorrect.
 
A large A in front of a memory indicates that it is aural (sounds only). A large V in front indicates the memory is visual (you see different footage).
 
 
Tomahna

Atrus' Workshop

A The necklace provides a memory of Atrus reading his journal (found in the top drawer of the desk).

A Zoom in on the crystal viewer. Atrus is explaining to Catherine that they wont even need to go to Rime anymore as they can view it from here.

A Zoom in on the 3 security cameras and hear Atrus placate Catherine's fear that his new security cameras will make her feel 'scrutinized' by telling her that he will only put them at the most likely entrance points.

A Zoom in on the telescope. Atrus points out to Yeesha a star that his grandmother Anna used to navigate by.


The flute playing/family tree room

A Zoom in on the family tree stone and hear Atrus telling Yeesha how this is only part of their family tree, as they can trace themselves back as far as their ancestors arriving in D'ni.


The Botany Lab

A Zoom in on Yeesha's bug tank and you will hear a memory of her remonstrating with Atrus not to touch it or he will ruin her experiment.

A Zoom in on the control box - Catherine complains to Atrus that the power has blown 5 times that week and that they need a back up system. What if someone gets stuck in the lift?

A Zoom in on the dirt and leaves on the floor next to the white door and you will hear sounds of a struggle with something being broken and Yeesha sounding distressed - "I have the key, I have the key! Please!"

V Zoom in on the healthy white flower in the incubator and you will see the plant looking worse for wear and hear Catherine saying how it didn't do well in Sirrus' garden but that after a few days here, it should be fine.


Atrus and Catherine's bedroom

V When you first pick up the necklace inside the fireplace in Atrus' and Catherine's bedroom, you can view a memory of Yeesha being chased through the bedroom and just managing to lock herself in the fireplace as a hand reaches in to grab her.

A Zoom in on the painting above the fireplace and the memory will be Catherine reminiscing about that day on Myst island when her sons were young and asking Atrus if they did the right thing (presumably in locking the boys away on Spire and Haven).

A Hidden in the left-hand secret slot on Atrus' desk is his journal on Spire. There is a necklace memory of Atrus reading of that journal.

A Hidden in the right-hand secret slot on Atrus' desk is his journal on Haven. There is a necklace memory of Atrus reading of that journal.


The kitchen

V Zoom in on the language book and you will see Atrus teaching Yeesha how to make a link to another age.

A Zoom in on the stove and you will hear Catherine suggesting to Yeesha that they use the special plates as her father is linking home that night.

A Zoom in on the pots. Yeesha suggests that Atrus doesn't make this meal too often and perhaps they should hide the pan before Catherine gets home.

A Zoom in on the chess board and hear Atrus surmising how he might beat Sirrus (presumably) in this chess game.


In Yeesha's room

A The necklace provides a memory of Yeesha reading her journal (found on the bed).

A The crystal statue on the desk plays a memory of Yeesha telling Catherine that Sirrus made it for her after she told him what a water spirit guide looks like.

A Click on the hanging dress and hear Yeesha pleading with Catherine to wear the dress today. Catherine tells her to wait until the ceremony next week when Anya will give her a necklace.

A Zoom in on the left-side of the bookcase (where the glass is broken) and hear that it was Achenar, because he 'knew you'd have one'. (You need to have solved the bookcase puzzle and gone to either Spire or Haven before this memory appears).


The Golden Linking Sphere

A Zoom in on the broken door and hear Atrus promise Catherine that they will see their sons after he has installed the security seals in the linking chambers.


Walkways

A The plaque on the balcony outside Atrus' and Catherine's bedroom has a memory of Catherine thanking Atrus for everything and noting that, for all the mistakes they have made, the ending has not yet been written.

V To see the memory of your trip into Tomahna with Yeesha, go back outside Atrus' workshop to where you arrived on the Mag Lev and zoom in on the lever.


Atrus' study and balcony

V When you link back to Tomahna from the arrival point in Serenia, you end up on the balcony in Tomahna that you first saw in Myst III Exile. Click on the bench with the shawl and the necklace will show you the memory of Catherine with baby Yeesha.

V In Atrus' study, the necklace provides a memory of Atrus reading his journal (found on the desk).

V Click on the feather on his desk and see his original letter asking you to help (no, there wasn't any mention of payment!).

V Click on the left side tapestry and revisit Saavedro stealing the Releeshan linking book.

A Click on the right hand tapestry to hear Catherine and Atrus discussing the 'redesign' of the room.


Spire


Up the top

A Zoom in on the journal on the 'prisoner' side of the ruined linking chamber to hear Yeesha offering to teach Sirrus D'ni.

V One step outside of the linking chamber, turn around and zoom in on the roof to see the chamber just before the explosion and hear it happen.

A The crystal statues of the family play a memory of Sirrus trying to recall the painting in Atrus and Catherine's bedroom.

V Click on the blanket and old fire near the crystal statues and see Sirrus cursing Atrus and trying to think how he will get out without a linking book.

V Zoom in on the metal ruler below the first large crystal you see to view the pentagonal rock channel it sits in.

A Zoom in on the hole of the first rock tunnel slide and hear Sirrus talking himself through tying the knot on the rope and hoping he will find the linking book at the bottom.


Ship docking area

A Zoom in on the console to hear Sirrus having a second go, after losing his first ship.

A Open the circuit board and Sirrus will gloat about how he had to build everything from scratch but that the lessons he got from Atrus on Voltaic helped.

A Stand out on the edge and then zoom in on the other island. You'll hear Sirrus celebrating his discovery that there is enough static electricity out there to be gathered by his conductors.


The living areas

A On the middle level of Spire near the climbing pole, click on the very large piece of chained, floating rock to hear the memory of Sirrus' reaction to it.

A Look through the telescope in Sirrus' laboratory on the middle level to hear him deciding that if he can get across to the other island, he may be able to get to the ground.

A Zoom in on the experiments next to the telescope to hear Sirrus discovering the properties of the crystals.

A The necklace provides a memory of Sirrus reading his journal (found on the lower table near the experiments and telescope).

A Zoom in on the flower held by the statue of Catherine in the garden area and you'll hear Sirrus' bemused reaction to his mother giving him another plant.

A Zoom in on the large crystal set in the hexagonal rock and hear that Sirrus was on experiment number 29 when the explosion that tore out the metal ruler and embedded it in the bed happened.

A Click on the chess set and hear Sirrus scoffing at his father's last move. You also learn that he got the Nara stone as chess pieces from Atrus.


A Zoom in on the upper console on Spire to hear Sirrus cursing himself for not aligning the magnetic forces perfectly to get the ship out of dock.


The other island/palace

V Zoom in on the 'down pipe' to view Sirrus climbing down a rope to 'freedom'.

V Zoom in on the viewer in the centre of the net of chains to see Sirrus discovering that freedom isn't so close after all.

A Zoom in on the console to hear Sirrus talking about how he needs power to flow through to the throne. It's the only way to reach the right frequency.

A Open the circuit board and the necklace memory is Sirrus talking about how there is lots of electricity out there that will go through the throne to where it is needed most. Can you hear the music, father? (Do you still hear the lambs, Clarice?)


Tuning Spire

A On the first level down from the throne room, zoom in on the statue of Catherine and Atrus to hear Sirrus warning his father how much 'a smart little girl can do!'

A At the lowest point below Spire, zoom in on the tools on the tray to your right. Sirrus complains that when the wires slip out of tune, they sound worse than 'Mother's caterwauling'


The crystal platform

A The necklace provides a memory of Sirrus reading his journal (found in the bench).

A Zoom in on the small hand-cranked generator. Sirrus talks about how the matrix wants to hold onto the power indefinitely but loses the power the instant it touches a grounded object. He wonders how he can isolate the crystals until he needs to use them.

V Zoom in on the left hand containment unit and the necklace will show you a memory of a rock crystal being delivered to the unit and then collected in the basket.

V Zoom in on the right hand containment unit and the necklace will show you a memory of a nara crystal being delivered to the unit and then collected in the basket.


V Once you have completed Spire you can go back to the elevator with the medallion and the necklace will show you the memory of that final ride back up to the top.
