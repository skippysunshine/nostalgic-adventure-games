Start with the console at the very top of the ladder.

Reset the console by opening and closing the toggle switch on the left and move the slider:

left, then center, then right.

Power is transferred from two of the floating crystals to the upper electromagnet. After a moment the pod will rise up and stick to the upper electromagnet.
         
         

Turn around and climb down the ladder to the lower console. Press the blue button to open the lid and then flip down the toggle switch. This slider has also has 3 positions but you have 5 adjustments before power is transferred from the crystals floating in front of you.
         

Reset the console by opening and closing the switch on the left and move the slider:

right, center, right, center, right.

The power transfers from the 7 floating crystals to the lower electromagnet. This will give you a difference of 5 between the upper and lower electromagnets and the pod should now float down gently to the correct level and a metal walkway will extend out to it.

Before you go for a ride, check the printed circuit board and you will see that you have managed to light up a few of those....... thingys.
